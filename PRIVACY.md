# Privacy Policy — Manga Fit Height

_Last updated: 2026-07-18_

## Summary

Manga Fit Height does **not** collect, store on remote servers, transmit, share
or sell any personal data. It has no analytics, no tracking and no network
requests of its own.

## What the extension stores

The only data the extension keeps are **your own settings** — for each site: the
CSS selector you picked, minimum image width, header offset, and the focus /
navigation / dimming toggles, plus your chosen interface language.

These are saved using Chrome's `storage.sync` API. That means they live in your
browser profile and, if you are signed in to the browser, may be synced across
your own devices by the browser itself. The extension never sends this data
anywhere else.

## Permissions and why they are needed

- **storage** — to save your per-site settings.
- **activeTab** / **scripting** — to read the selector matches in the current
  tab and apply the styling when you interact with the popup.
- **host access (`<all_urls>`)** — manga readers live on many different domains,
  and you decide per site whether the extension is active. The extension only
  changes how images are displayed; it does not read page content, form data,
  cookies or credentials, and does not send anything from the pages you visit.

## Data sharing

None. No third parties receive any data.
